## What shipped

- The server layer per §9: the `ActionResult` envelope, zod validators, and ten
  marketplace actions — `createJob` (margin derived, the money invariant
  re-checked); `placeBid` / `updateBid` / `withdrawBid` (pool-remainder math via
  `lib/money`, optimistic placement reconciling on the action result);
  `acceptBid` / `rejectBid` (Serializable acceptance: `JobMember`,
  `BID_ACCEPTED` XP with tier recompute, OPEN → ASSIGNED, the job channel
  provisioned); `startJob` / `submitForReview` / `requestChanges` (notes post to
  the job channel); and the completion transaction (earnings incremented by
  accepted splits, `JOB_COMPLETED` XP, tier recompute).
- Conditional writes close the read-check-write races an adversarial audit
  constructed against bid edit/withdraw/reject; acceptance is limited to OPEN and
  ASSIGNED per §7.1; a single P2034 retry absorbs routine serialization aborts.
- Reads moved to `lib/queries` with serialized Decimals and dates. The rail,
  channel pages, and the Today view left the mock for the database so
  provisioned job channels are reachable; channel visibility derives from
  `lib/channels.ts` (PRD 7.3 as amended). Owner rulings folded into PRD.md.
