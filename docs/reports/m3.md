## What shipped

- The catalog grouped by department with thin token-colored progress bars
  derived from `LessonCompletion` rows; a two-pane classroom with the ordered
  outline and completion ticks; the lesson reader with one optimistic
  mark-complete control that becomes a muted completed-date line and reconciles
  on the action result.
- `completeLesson` applies the post-M2 conditional-write rule end to end: the
  enrollment row lock is taken before counting (so concurrent completions
  serialize and the final-lesson bonus can neither double-fire nor be skipped),
  the completion insert is a `skipDuplicates` no-op on repeat, progress recomputes
  from completions, and `COURSE_COMPLETED` awards exactly once via the
  `isCompleted` flip. The transaction body lives in `lib/academy.ts` so the §12
  idempotent-lesson-award invariant is tested against the real database —
  including a last-two-lessons race.
- The quiet toast layer (raised panel, 4s, no confetti): tier-ups fire at the
  boundary in gold mono, course completion echoes its bonus. Tier badges render
  beside names on every database-backed surface — top bar, chat senders, bidders,
  workers, the Today activity — never on clients or system users. The profile
  page reads everything from real rows: ledger-derived figures, completed work
  with accounts, courses finished, the reverse-chrono `XpEvent` record. §9
  amended per rulings (the conditional-write rule; `lib/xp` and `lib/progression`
  named).
